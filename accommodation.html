Accommodation

There are plenty of options for accommodation near the venue. If you're looking for the easiest option, we have a room block at Hotel Indigo in Traverse City, and a shuttle to take guests to the venue and return you at the end of the night!

Given the popularity of the area in the summertime, we do encourage guests to book accommodation as early as possible!

Hotel Indigo, Traverse City

Right on the West Bay, Hotel Indigo is an easy walk into town, and one of the closest hotels to the venue (roughly 15 minutes, driving). Don't forget to use the Group Code MWW for access to the block!

Baywatch Resort, Traverse City

Also right on the water, but further round the the bay. Baywatch Resort is about 25 minutes from the venue.

Vacation Rentals

Longer stays, larger groups, or even just something a little different... both Airbnb and Vrbo have a bunch of great options. Ideal locations close to the venue:

  • Traverse City
  • Suttons Bay
  • Leelanau
